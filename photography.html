Photography

I love to explore new places. To name a few, I've been to various locations in India, like Udaipur, Delhi, Chennai, Kerela, Puducherry. I've also visited a few countries like Bhutan, Vietnam, Thailand & Dubai. The plan is to travel to Europe next.

I take random photos of the places I visit and nowhere consider myself near to pro. As of now, I am learning to set up my DSLR in the right way and learning Lightroom as well. Below is my photography arsenal:

Google Pixel 2 (Retired), iPhone XS Max, Go Pro Hero 7 Black, Nikon D5200 (18-55mm), Mi Selfie Stick Tripod, BT-150 Bosch Tripod, Fujifilm Instax Mini 9 Instant (Polaroid)
